Features

Six decisions, one philosophy

Every feature exists to protect your attention — not to capture it.

Consent Halo

Signature

A soft lilac light, built into the frame and visible to others, not to you. It turns privacy into a promise people can see — the opposite of a hidden camera.

Bone-conduction audio

Sound travels through bone, straight to you. Bystanders hear nothing, your ears stay open to the world. No earbuds, no isolation.

A single button

One discreet control on the temple to turn guidance on or off. Findable by touch, never triggered by accident.

Lisia-direct

The glasses connect straight to the Lisia app. Base keeps your phone nearby; Pro runs fully phone-free with on-device intelligence.

All-day battery

A minimum of eight hours of active use — enough to wear them like glasses, not like a gadget you have to babysit.

No camera

By design, and without exception. There is no housing for a lens, in any version. What you see stays yours.

About

Calm technology, made wearable

Loopi is the wearable layer of Lisia — a system built on a simple belief: the best technology asks for nothing.

What we believe

Wellbeing apps were meant to help, then started to monitor. Notifications became guilt. Tracking became pressure. We think a tool for your wellbeing should leave you alone — and still be there when you need it.

What Loopi is

Smart glasses that guide quietly and protect your privacy visibly. No camera, no feed, no fight for your attention. Designed in the language of eyewear, not electronics.

Where it comes from

Built within the Lisia ecosystem and shaped by people who felt watched by their own wellness apps. Recognised by Harvard Innovation Labs, VML Future 100 and Forbes.
